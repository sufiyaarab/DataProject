Explore Our Health Insurance Subpolicies

Health insurance is essential for your well-being. Explore the various subpolicies we offer to protect your health and your finances.

Dental Coverage

Dental insurance ensures your oral health is always a priority, covering both routine and advanced procedures.

Coverage Includes:

  • Routine check-ups and cleanings (every 6 months).
  • Coverage for X-rays and diagnostic exams.
  • Support for major procedures such as root canals, crowns, and orthodontics (e.g., braces).

Additional Features:

  • Coverage tiers for basic care vs. advanced dental work.
  • Preventative care often covered 100% in-network.
  • Option to add orthodontic coverage for children and adults.
  • Specialized plans for cosmetic dental work.

Preventative Coverage

Preventative health coverage promotes long-term well-being by focusing on early detection and wellness strategies.

Coverage Includes:

  • Vaccinations for children and adults (e.g., flu, HPV, COVID-19).
  • Annual physical exams and wellness check-ups.
  • Screenings for cancer, diabetes, cholesterol, and blood pressure.

Why It’s Important:

  • Reduces the risk of developing chronic illnesses through early intervention.
  • Encourages regular health monitoring and proactive care.
  • Improves overall health outcomes by identifying potential issues early.
  • Helps lower long-term healthcare costs by preventing severe conditions.

Vision Coverage

Vision insurance helps reduce the costs of maintaining your eye health and managing vision correction.

What’s Covered:

  • Annual eye exams, often fully covered.
  • Discounts or full coverage for prescription glasses and lenses.
  • Contact lenses and fittings.
  • Glaucoma and cataract screenings.

Additional Benefits:

  • Discounts on lens enhancements like anti-glare coatings.
  • Coverage for pediatric vision care, including corrective lenses.

Why It’s Important:

  • Supports timely detection and treatment of eye conditions.
  • Helps manage the costs of prescription eyewear for families.

Prescription Coverage

Prescription insurance provides essential access to medications prescribed by doctors, reducing out-of-pocket expenses.

Coverage Includes:

  • Generic, brand-name, and specialty medications.
  • Coverage for long-term prescriptions (e.g., for chronic conditions).
  • Vaccines and preventative treatments.

Why Choose Prescription Coverage:

  • Ensures access to vital medications without financial strain.
  • Wide pharmacy network for easy fulfillment of prescriptions.
  • Encourages adherence to prescribed treatments for better health outcomes.
  • Includes discount programs for high-cost specialty drugs.

Terms and Conditions

Health

Covered Services: Includes doctor visits, hospital stays, surgeries, and diagnostic tests as outlined in the policy.

Network Providers: Policyholders must use providers within the insurer’s network to receive full benefits.

Pre-Existing Conditions: Coverage for pre-existing conditions may be excluded unless specified in the policy.

Preventative Services: Regular checkups, screenings, and vaccinations are covered as per policy guidelines.

Dental

Scope of Coverage: Covers routine dental cleanings, fillings, and basic procedures.

Exclusions: Cosmetic treatments such as veneers or teeth whitening are not covered unless specified.

Maximum Coverage: Annual limits on benefits apply, and unused amounts do not roll over to the next year.

Orthodontics: Braces and other orthodontic treatments may require a separate rider or additional premium.

Preventative Care

Covered Services: Includes immunizations, screenings, and counseling to prevent illness or injury.

Exclusions: Experimental treatments or procedures not approved by the insurer are excluded.

Policyholder Responsibilities: Annual wellness visits must be scheduled within the coverage year.

Network Requirements: Coverage is limited to services provided by in-network providers.

Vision

Coverage Includes: Annual eye exams, prescription glasses, and contact lenses (if applicable).

Exclusions: Coverage does not include elective procedures like LASIK unless specifically covered.

Claim Requirements: Claims must include a prescription and proof of purchase.

Limits: Frames, lenses, or contacts are covered up to a specified amount annually.

Prescription Coverage

Covered Medications: Includes prescription drugs listed on the insurer’s formulary.

Exclusions: Over-the-counter medications, supplements, and experimental drugs are not covered.

Co-Payments: The policyholder is responsible for co-pays as outlined in the policy.

Generic Substitutions: Insurers may require the use of generic drugs when available.
